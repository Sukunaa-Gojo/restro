Our Philosophy

Crafted with
Intention,
Served with Soul

Lumière was born from a simple belief — that a café can be more than a coffee stop. It can be a pause, a breath, a place where time softens and every detail is considered. Our sourcing is obsessive, our preparation is unhurried, and our welcome is always warm.

From single-origin espresso pulled with precision to pastries made fresh before dawn, we bring the art of the Parisian salon to the heart of Mumbai.

— Chef Isabelle Laurent
